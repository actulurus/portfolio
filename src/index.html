Why choose me

over other UI designers?

UI Element structure

Ever ordered a UI and it was a mess to work with? Did the buttons have a {0, 0} AnchorPoint, resulting in them not animating correctly? Were there 4 elements with the same name?
It's happened to me too, and that's why I structure my UIs in a way that's easy to work with. I use the correct class for each element and they are named depending on what they do.

Imported & scaled

All of my UIs are imported into Roblox and scale correctly on all devices, with that being included in the price. I avoid designing the entire interface in an external program, but when it's necessary, I use Inkscape (SVGs).

Good communication

Before starting, we discuss how the UIs should look (style, layout, colors, ...). A price is agreed upon and I get to work.
During the designing process, I ask my clients for feedback and fix anything that they don't like.
When done, I allow the client to make any final tweaks and then proceed to payment.
